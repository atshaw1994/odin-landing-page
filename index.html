Odin
Odin is a prominent figure in Norse mythology and Germanic paganism. He is widely revered as a god associated with wisdom, magic, death, divination, and poetry. He is often depicted as a one-eyed, old man with a long beard, and is known as the Allfather. He is associated with Valhalla, the hall where fallen warriors go after death.
Odin was the god of:
Wisdom
Magic
Death
War
